Enemy models in the Pulse must pass a Size Challenge Test (7), or be moved directly away from this model until they hit an Impassable terrain feature, a model which passes its Size Challenge Test or until they have moved 8".
If another model is contacted by models being moved by this feat, it must make a Size Challenge (6) Test or be pushed along with the original model.
